**kbark** is a Kubernetes terminal UI in the spirit of k9s — with one new key.

Press **`?`** on any pod, deployment, log line, or event, and kbark pulls the context for you, streams a diagnosis inline, and tells you the cause in plain English. Read-only. Single binary. Bring your own key (Anthropic, OpenAI, or Ollama).
